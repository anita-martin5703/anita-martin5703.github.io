## Anita Martin

My name is Anita Martin. I currently a student with the Central New Mexico Community College STEMlus Center. I have been working in the warehouse industry for the past decade. I recently have changed up gears and decided to pursue a long passion of mine, computers. Right after High School I immediately wanted to go into the Computer Field, so I decided to go to ITT Technical Insititute in Albuquerque, New Mexico. After two years I received my Associates Degree in Information Technology. When I started school I also started as an hourly employee with Dollar Tree. After about a year I worked my way up to an Assistant Store Manager. Just about the same time as I graduated I also was being promoted to becoming a Store Manager and had the opportunity to help with the opening of the newest store in our district out in Gallup, New Mexico. This was an exciting time for me as I was the youngest out of all the managers in our district and having a brand new store to run I had a lot of eyes and pressure on me. The first year our store was number one each month beating our quotas for sales and hours. With all the excitment and the nice salary and the added bonuses each month my degree was put on the back burner and only in my spare time did I even think about computers. It was not until I started with DISH back in September of 2009 that my love for computers came to the forefront again. With DISH I was able to apply some of my knowledge and skills that I had learned back in college and use that to my advantage.
